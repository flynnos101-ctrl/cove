About Cove

Cove is an AI implementation marketplace built in Sydney, Australia. We connect businesses that need AI solutions with vetted specialists who can deliver them.

The problem

Most businesses know they need AI but don't know where to start. Hiring in-house is expensive. Freelancers are unreliable. Consulting firms charge $500/hr to tell you what you already know. There's no marketplace that vets for quality, holds funds in escrow, and guarantees outcomes.

Our approach

We reject 94% of applicants. Every operator on Cove has been vetted through technical assessment, portfolio review, and reference checks. Payments are escrow-protected. Contracts are auto-generated. You pay for outcomes, not hours.

Based in Sydney

Cove is an Australian company governed by NSW law. Our platform fee is 15% and covers vetting, matching, escrow management, contract generation, and dispute mediation.

Pricing

Simple, transparent pricing. No hidden fees.

Platform Fee
Applied to each project
15%

How escrow works: When you submit a brief, your budget is held in escrow via Stripe. The operator cannot access the funds. When you approve the delivery, Cove releases 85% to the operator and retains 15%.

What's included: Vetting and matching, auto-generated contracts, escrow management, in-platform messaging, progress tracking, and dispute mediation.

Refund policy: Full refund if the project hasn't started. Pro-rata payment for completed work on cancellations. 7 days written notice required.

Terms of Service

1. Platform - Cove Pty Ltd operates an AI implementation marketplace. Cove facilitates but is not party to service delivery.

2. Accounts - Users must provide accurate information and maintain credential security.

3. Payments - Escrow via Stripe. 15% platform fee. Released upon client approval.

4. IP - Client owns project outputs on payment. Operators retain pre-existing tools.

5. Disputes - Mediated by Cove within 14 days. Governed by NSW law.

6. Cancellation - 7 days notice. Completed work paid pro-rata.

7. Liability - Capped at escrow value. No consequential damages.

8. Modifications - 30 days notice for term changes.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection - Name, email, company info, project details collected for platform services.

Payments - Processed by Stripe. No card details stored by Cove.

Communications - Platform messages stored for project delivery and disputes.

Sharing - Never sold. Shared only between matched parties.

Retention - Account data for life of account. Project data 2 years post-completion.

Rights - Request export or deletion via support at cove dot au.

Jurisdiction - Australian Privacy Act 1988.
