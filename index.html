Frequently asked.

What is your refund policy?expand_more
Funds are held in escrow until milestones are approved. If a project is cancelled before work begins, you receive a full refund. For in-progress work, completed milestones are paid and remaining funds returned.
How long does matching take?expand_more
Most briefs are matched within 48 hours. Complex or highly specialised projects may take up to 5 business days.
Can I switch operators?expand_more
Yes. If you're unsatisfied with your match, contact Cove within 7 days and we'll reassign at no extra cost.
What are the fees?expand_more
Cove charges a 15% platform fee on top of the project price. This covers vetting, escrow, contract generation, and dispute mediation.
Who owns the IP?expand_more
All project deliverables are owned by the client upon full payment. Operators retain pre-existing tools and reusable frameworks.
Do you work with enterprise?expand_more
Cove is designed for SMEs but we work with teams of all sizes. For enterprise needs, use the Inquire button and we'll scope a custom engagement.

About Cove

Cove is an AI implementation marketplace built in Sydney, Australia. We connect businesses that need AI solutions with vetted specialists who can deliver them.

The problem

Most businesses know they need AI but don't know where to start. Hiring in-house is expensive. Freelancers are unreliable. Consulting firms charge $500/hr to tell you what you already know.

Our approach

We reject 94% of applicants. Every operator on Cove has been vetted through technical assessment, portfolio review, and reference checks. Payments are escrow-protected. Contracts are auto-generated. You pay for outcomes, not hours.

Based in Sydney

Cove is an Australian company governed by NSW law.

Pricing

Cove charges a 15% platform fee on all projects. This covers vetting, escrow, contracts, and dispute mediation.

How escrow works

When you start a project, funds are held securely via Stripe. They are only released to the operator when you approve the deliverables. If you cancel before work begins, you get a full refund.

Service price ranges

Workflow Automation: $3k-$15k • Lead Generation: $5k-$20k • Customer Support AI: $5k-$25k • Sales Automation: $4k-$18k • Marketing Automation: $3k-$12k • Data Infrastructure: $8k-$40k • AI Strategy: $2k-$10k • Custom Builds: $10k-$100k+

Terms of Service

1. Platform — Cove Pty Ltd operates an AI marketplace connecting clients with vetted operators. Cove facilitates but is not party to service delivery.

2. Payments — All funds held in escrow via Stripe. 15% platform fee. Released upon client approval of deliverables.

3. IP — Project outputs owned by Client on full payment. Operators retain pre-existing tools and reusable frameworks.

4. Disputes — Mediated by Cove within 14 business days. Governed by laws of New South Wales, Australia.

5. Cancellation — 7 days written notice. Completed work paid pro-rata from escrow.

6. Liability — Capped at total escrow value. No indirect or consequential damages.

7. Accounts — Users must provide accurate information and maintain credential security.

8. Amendments — Cove may update these terms with 30 days notice. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection — We collect name, email, company information, and project details to operate platform services.

Payment Data — Payment processing handled by Stripe. Cove does not store credit card or bank details.

Data Sharing — We never sell personal data. Information shared only between matched clients and operators.

Retention — Account data retained for life of account. Project data retained 2 years after completion.

Cookies — We use essential cookies for session management. No third-party tracking cookies.

Your Rights — Request data export or deletion via support at cove dot au.

Jurisdiction — Governed by Australian Privacy Act 1988.
